Lunar New Years

In the night, the Moon will rise
Celebrations and cheers begin,
Wishes, Good Forturne, and Sincere Surprises
Freeness beholds the kin.



Eat food, drink drinks, feel the joy
Play games with those around,
Exchange the gifts of dollars and coins,
And be immersed in the chatter sounds.



But remember the elders who care and love
for the generations they haved reared,
Bow to them and those above,
Remember their labor and tears.



Pray to the gods for a good life
Pray for fortunes to reign,
Hope for peace and reject bleak strife
Life's Journey is not in vain.



The Sun will turn but the Moon will stay
Like a gold tocken it shines,
Red and Gold will light the way
So wonderful and so divine.
